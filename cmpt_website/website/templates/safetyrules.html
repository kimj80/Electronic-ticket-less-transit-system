Emergency Phones and Buttons.

JDK Transit has placed BLUE terminal phones for emergencies, all across our ETS and LRT transit routes.

By pressing the button on the device, you will be automatically connected to a live personnel at the EPS emergency call center.

Try and voice your emgergency as clearly as possible, each device is equipped with a GPS to for us to send over coordinates to our emergency staff asap.

Phones are always avaialble and active 24/7, if you notice a terminal is damaged or isn't working properly please call 311 and report the device.

Closed Circuit Television.

JDK Transit has the rights to record and monitor suspicious behaviours on our transit system.

You as our consumer can also request access to any of our data, by emaling us the date, time and bus number.

Videos constantly viewed daily by our latest AI system to detect suspicious activity, sponsored by our parent company "Minority Report"
